Our Story

From Seed to Cup,
We Care About Every Step

What started as a tiny roastery in a garage has grown into a beloved neighborhood fixture. We source directly from farmers in Nuwara Eliya, Kandy and Badulla—paying fair prices and building relationships that span years.

Every batch is roasted in small lots right here in our shop. You can smell it from the sidewalk, and that's exactly how we like it.
